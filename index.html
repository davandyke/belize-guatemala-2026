Monday, March 16 — Tikal National Park → Flores

  • 4:00–8:00 AM — Sunrise Tour ($16/person + park entry) — confirming with hotel
  • Full morning exploring the ruins before day-tripper crowds arrive and heat builds
  • Check out by 11:00 AM — shuttle from lodge to Flores island (~1 hr)
  • Check into accommodation in Flores
  • Evening: lakeside dinner in Flores — a proper meal and cold drinks to close out Guatemala
Tip

Book your Flores → San Ignacio shuttle for March 17 before leaving for Tikal. Shuttles run from Santa Elena/Flores and fill up.

Tuesday, March 17 — Flores → San Ignacio, Belize

  • Morning: Depart Flores toward the Belize border
  • Cross at Benque Viejo / Melchor de Mencos border crossing
  • Arrive San Ignacio, Belize — check into accommodation
  • Afternoon: Explore San Ignacio town, Cahal Pech ruins, or rest

Wednesday, March 18 — ATM Cave (Actun Tunichil Muknal)

Warning

Advance booking required — ATM Cave tours must be booked with a licensed guide. Confirm your guide reservation before departure.

  • Full-day tour to ATM Cave
  • Swim and hike through the cave system
  • See ancient Mayan artifacts and the famous “Crystal Maiden” skeleton
  • Return to San Ignacio by late afternoon

Thursday, March 19 — San Ignacio → Belize City (Travel Buffer)

  • Morning: Depart San Ignacio toward Belize City (~2 hrs by bus or shuttle)
  • Afternoon: Explore Belize City or relax before island transfer
  • Evening: Optional — stay overnight in Belize City for early departure to Caye Caulker

Friday, March 20 — Arrive Caye Caulker

  • Water taxi from Belize City Marine Terminal to Caye Caulker (~45 min)
  • Check-in: 3:00 PM — Airbnb hosted by Norm, Playa Asuncion
  • Confirmation: HMDYZAD4J2
  • Evening: Sunset at The Split, dinner on the island

Saturday–Tuesday, March 21–24 — Caye Caulker

Days are yours to enjoy island life.

Suggested activities:

  • Snorkeling at Hol Chan Marine Reserve & Shark Ray Alley
  • Manatee watching tour
  • Kayaking and paddleboarding
  • Exploring the village by bicycle
  • Lounging at The Split
