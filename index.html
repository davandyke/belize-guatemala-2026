Day-by-Day Itinerary

Sunday, March 15 — Travel Day (Grand Rapids → Tikal)

Flight 1 — United UA 2099

  • Depart: GRR (Grand Rapids) at 7:25 AM
  • Arrive: ORD (Chicago O’Hare) at 7:37 AM
  • Connection: 1h 24m

Flight 2 — United UA 2006

  • Depart: ORD (Chicago) at 9:01 AM
  • Arrive: GUA (Guatemala City) at 12:41 PM
Warning

Tight connection in Guatemala City: ~2 hours between landing and TagAirlines check-in at 2:40 PM. Clear customs and immigration quickly — do not check bags through to Flores if possible.

Tip

Cash: Pull Q800 from the ATM at Guatemala City airport on arrival — covers the hostel plus meals in Flores.

Flight 3 — TagAirlines 5U150

  • Check-in: 2:40 PM at GUA
  • Depart: GUA at 4:10 PM
  • Arrive: FRS (Flores) at 5:10 PM

Evening: Arrive FRS ~5:10 PM. Take lodge shuttle to Tikal (~1 hr). Check in, dinner at the lodge, sleep to howler monkeys.

Jungle Lodge — Reservation #2041175675302

  • Status: ⚠️ Pending confirmation — follow up if no response by March 3
  • Payment: Full amount charged automatically ~March 10 — free cancellation until March 10
  • Shuttle: FRS airport → lodge ($19/person — confirm with hotel)
  • Contact: info@junglelodgetikal.com | WhatsApp +502 3185 0223

Monday, March 16 — Tikal National Park → Flores

  • 4:00–8:00 AM — Sunrise Tour ($16/person + ~$20 park entry cash at gate) — confirm with hotel

  • Full morning in the ruins — climb temples, explore the jungle, watch for wildlife

  • Check out by 11:00 AM — shuttle from lodge to Flores island (~1 hr)

  • Check into Los Amigos Hostel — Booking ID #45456, pay Q600 cash at check-in

  • King room with lake and jungle views

  • Evening: lakeside dinner in Flores — cold drinks and a proper meal to close out Guatemala

Tip

Tonight: Book your Flores → San Ignacio shuttle at the Los Amigos front desk. Shuttles depart from Santa Elena and fill up — easiest to arrange through the hostel.

Tuesday, March 17 — Flores → San Ignacio, Belize

  • Morning: Shuttle from Flores/Santa Elena toward the Belize border (~$25-35, 3-4 hrs including border crossing)
  • Cross at Benque Viejo / Melchor de Mencos
  • Arrive San Ignacio — check into Bella’s Backpackers, Booking #44298
    • 4 Galves St, San Ignacio | Private room with private bathroom
    • WhatsApp: +501 632-8868
  • Afternoon: Take it easy or pick one activity:
    • Cahal Pech ruins — walking distance, small well-preserved Mayan site, easy half day
    • Xunantunich ruins — 30 min away, massive site with El Castillo pyramid and incredible views, hand-cranked river ferry
    • Tubing on the Macal or Mopan River — lazy jungle float, great way to meet people

Wednesday, March 18 — ATM Cave (Actun Tunichil Muknal)

Mayawalk Tours — Order #R0SAMLP ✅ Confirmed

  • Meet at Mayawalk office, 19 Burns Avenue, San Ignacio at 8:00 AM
  • 45-min drive to Tapir Mountain Nature Reserve, 45-min jungle hike to cave entrance
  • ~3 hours inside — swimming, wading, climbing through chambers to the Crystal Maiden
  • Lunch included (non-vegetarian), back in San Ignacio by ~4:30 PM
  • Balance due on day: $73.77
Tip

What to wear/bring: Old closed-toe shoes (will get soaked — pack flip flops to change into), socks mandatory. No cameras allowed in the cave. Bring your printed waiver and ID.

Thursday, March 19 — San Ignacio Free Day

  • Rest or choose an activity:
    • Xunantunich — if not done March 17, half day, easy to do independently
    • Barton Creek Cave — canoe through a cave with Mayan artifacts and skulls, serene and eerie, 3-4 hrs small group
    • Tubing — lazy river through jungle, social, easy to arrange through Bella’s
  • Evening: Last night in San Ignacio — dinner and drinks on Burns Avenue

Friday, March 20 — San Ignacio → Caye Caulker

  • ~11:00–11:30 AM — Depart San Ignacio by shuttle (~2 hrs)
  • ~1:00–1:30 PM — Arrive Belize City Marine Terminal
  • ~2:00 PM — Water taxi to Caye Caulker (~45 min) — buy ticket on the day
  • 3:00 PM — Check in at Airbnb hosted by Norm, Playa Asuncion | Confirmation: HMDYZAD4J2
  • Evening: Sunset at The Split, dinner on the island

Saturday–Tuesday, March 21–24 — Caye Caulker

Relax at the Airbnb, dive, and enjoy the island.

Diving — Great Blue Hole & Other Trips ⭐ Book before arrival — trips run twice a week and fill fast in March

  • Full day, 3-tank dive: Blue Hole + Half Moon Caye Wall + Long Caye Aquarium

  • Departs ~6:00 AM, returns ~4:00 PM | ~$225-300 USD all inclusive

  • Frenchie’s Diving — since 1981, slightly cheaper (~$225). frenchiesdivingbelize.com

  • Sunrise Scuba — newer, highly rated, personal service. sunrisescubabelize.com

Other activities:

  • Snorkeling at Hol Chan Marine Reserve & Shark Ray Alley
  • The Split — swimming, cold drinks, good people-watching

Wednesday, March 25 — Fly Home

  • Check out: 10:00 AM from Airbnb
  • ~11:00–11:30 AM — Water taxi: Caye Caulker → Belize City (~45 min)
  • ~12:15–12:30 PM — Arrive Belize City, transfer to Philip Goldson International Airport (BZE)
